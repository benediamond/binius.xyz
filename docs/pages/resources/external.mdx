# External Resources [Content on Binius written by community members]

Here, we collect various posts and articles written by _external_ community members (outside of Irreducible) about Binius. If you'd like us to include your content here, please let us know.

- **Vitalik.** Vitalik Buterin has done an _extremely_ detailed [exposition](https://vitalik.eth.limo/general/2024/04/29/binius.html) of the main commitment scheme of [[DP23](https://eprint.iacr.org/2023/1784.pdf)]. He also [implemented](https://github.com/ethereum/research/blob/master/binius/simple_binius.py) that scheme in python.
- **Justin Thaler.** Justin Thaler of a16z crypto has written an [extensive article](https://a16zcrypto.com/posts/article/boosting-lassojolt/) on Binius, and moreover mentions it further in a [follow-up](https://a16zcrypto.com/posts/article/understanding-jolt-clarifications-and-reflections/).
- **LambdaClass.** _LambdaClass_ has done a two-part series on Binius; their posts can be seen [here](https://blog.lambdaclass.com/snarks-on-binary-fields-binius/) and [here](https://blog.lambdaclass.com/binius-part-2/).
- **L2Iterative.** L2Iterative has written a two posts, which respectively cover the papers [[DP23](https://eprint.iacr.org/2023/1784.pdf)] and [[DP24](https://eprint.iacr.org/2024/504.pdf)]. These posts are available [here](https://hackmd.io/@l2iterative/binius) and [here](https://hackmd.io/@l2iterative/binius2).
