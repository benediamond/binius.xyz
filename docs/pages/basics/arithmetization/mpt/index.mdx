# Merkle–Patricia Inclusion [A full description of our most important M3 instance]

In this section, we describe in detail our [M3-based](/basics/arithmetization/m3) arithmetization of _Merkle–Patricia inclusion_, a task of critical real-life importance in Ethereum. This arithmetization showcases the full power of M3, and demonstrates its capacity to solve real problems.
