# Talks and Lectures [Informal and academic video lectures on Binius]

Here, we collect various talks given by Irreducible team members on Binius.

- [**ZK Podcast.**](https://zeroknowledge.fm/303-2/) Here, Jim and Radi appear on the _Zero Knowledge Podcast_ with Anna Rose and Brendan Farmer, explaining some of the basic principles and ideas behind Binius.
- [**RISC Zero Study Club.**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8AMiZdWA1eM) Here, Ben joins the _RISC Zero Club_, discussing some mathematical background behind a coding-theoretic phenomenon called _proximity gaps_—and in particular the paper [[DP24](https://cic.iacr.org/p/1/1/2/pdf)].
- [**ZK Study Club: _Binius_.**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BeEuphrUipk) Here, Ben explains some aspects of the paper [[DP25](https://eprint.iacr.org/2024/1038.pdf)] in an academically-flavored talk with the _ZK Study Club_ lecture series.
- [**ZK Summit 11.**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rgRWcWOll0w) Here, Jim gives a talk about Binius at the main stage of ZK Summit 11 in Athens, Greece.
- [**Archetype's _Verifiable Compute Day_.**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rw8vg9Y_5NQ) Here, Jim gives a talk about Binius at venture firm Archetype's _Verifiable Compute Day_.
- [**ZK Study Club: _FRI-Binius_.**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iZlJDT7V8Q4) Here, Ben returns to ZK Study Club, this time discussing some aspects of the paper [[DP24](https://eprint.iacr.org/2024/504.pdf)].
